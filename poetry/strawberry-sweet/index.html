Strawberry Sweet

2024-05-25 by Isaac Freund

when summer's starting
and we are eating strawberries
you kiss me
and it is sweet

when rain is pouring
and bed is so inviting
in your arms
I feel complete

when times are trying
and life is falling out of balance
I am glad
that we did meet

when summer's ending
and there are no more strawberries
you kiss me
and it is sweet
