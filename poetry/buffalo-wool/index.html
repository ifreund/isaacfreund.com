Buffalo Wool

2025-05-26 by Isaac Freund

while walking in the mountains
after the last snow of the season
we see buffalo wool strewn about the meadows

eventually
we find the great beings themselves
one of them rolling in a dust bath
freeing itself of its winter coat

they watch us as we pass
giving them a wide berth
for we are small and weak in their eyes
uninvited guests in their lands
